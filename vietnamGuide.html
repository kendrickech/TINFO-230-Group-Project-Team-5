Discover the Beauty of Vietnam

Vietnam is a stunning country in Southeast Asia, offering breathtaking landscapes, a rich history, and vibrant street food culture. From the limestone cliffs of Ha Long Bay to the bustling streets of Ho Chi Minh City, every corner has something to explore.

Hanoi

Vietnam’s capital, full of history, vibrant markets, and the famous Old Quarter.

Key Attractions:

  • Hoan Kiem Lake: A peaceful lake in the heart of the city, home to the famous Turtle Tower.
  • Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum: The final resting place of Vietnam’s revered leader.
  • Old Quarter: A maze of narrow streets filled with street food, shops, and centuries-old architecture.
  • Temple of Literature: A historic university and a symbol of Hanoi’s rich educational heritage.

Ha Long Bay

A UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its emerald waters and limestone islands.

Key Attractions:

  • Boat Cruises: Explore the stunning bay on a traditional wooden junk boat.
  • Surprise Cave (Hang Sửng Sốt): One of the most impressive caves in Ha Long Bay.
  • Ti Top Island: Offers breathtaking panoramic views after a short hike.
  • Floating Fishing Villages: Visit local communities living on the water.

Hoi An

An ancient town famous for lantern-lit streets and tailor-made clothing.

Key Attractions:

  • Japanese Covered Bridge: A historic 18th-century bridge and a symbol of Hoi An.
  • Hoi An Night Market: A lively market with local crafts, souvenirs, and street food.
  • Tailor Shops: Get custom-made clothing from skilled local tailors.
  • My Son Sanctuary: An ancient Hindu temple complex, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Essential Travel Information

  • Language: Vietnamese.
  • Currency: Vietnamese Dong (VND).
  • Credit Cards & ATMs: ATMs are available in major cities.
  • Plugs: Types A, C, and F. Standard voltage is 220V.
  • Safety: Be cautious of phone snatching in Ho Chi Minh City.

Must-Try Vietnamese Dishes

  • Phở – Vietnamese noodle soup.
  • Bánh mì – Vietnamese sandwich.
  • Gỏi cuốn – Fresh spring rolls.
  • Bánh Xèo – Savory and Crispy Vietnamese pancake.
  • Bún Bò Huế – Spicy Beef Noodle Soup.
  • Bánh Bao – Vietnamese Steamed Bun.
